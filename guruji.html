Nirmal Guruji

Nirmal Guruji’s blessings and spiritual guidance are at the heart of our business. Through his grace and sacred Chola sets, The Mandir Company found inspiration and purpose. His devotion to humility, compassion, and unity continues to guide us in everything we create.

Something About Us

I always dreamed of having my own business, so I decided to start by making Guruji's chola sets and blessings. Today, The Mandir Company has expanded to provide all religions, offering Mandir items as well as home decor. As a token of gratitude, We have dedicated this page to him.

Who He Was

Nirmal Guruji, also known as Nirmal Singh Maharaj, was a revered spiritual leader and teacher from India. Born in 1952, he devoted his life to spreading spiritual knowledge, dharma, and compassion. Throughout his lifetime, he guided countless followers, emphasizing the importance of meditation, devotion, and selfless service. He passed away in 2007, leaving behind a legacy of teachings that continue to inspire millions.



Facts and Followers

  • Birth year: 1952

  • Death year: 2007

  • Followers: estimated in hundreds of thousands

  • Prominent countries with followers: India, United States, UK, Canada

  • Focus: spiritual teachings, meditation, Chola sets blessings, satsangs


Guruji’s teachings focused on inner peace, devotion, and spreading positivity. He inspired people across generations, helping them lead lives of discipline, spiritual awareness, and compassion. His blessings and guidance are especially important to The Mandir Company, as they have shaped the products and the philosophy behind the business.
